PLEASE NOTE:

Coastal Carolina University will issue up to six (6) official transcripts per student per calendar year at no charge. A non-refundable fee of $5 per copy, in check or money order, must accompany subsequent requests that are made within the same calendar year. Cash is not acceptable. Coastal Carolina reserves the right to alter this policy at any time.

Transcript(s) will not be released to/for anyone except the student, unless appropriately requested in writing by the student. Please present picture identification when requesting transcripts in person.
